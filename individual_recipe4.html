Tiramisu


Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Mascarpone
  • Coffee
  • Heavy Whipped Cream
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Vanila extract
  • Cocoa powder

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1: Mix creamy filling. Beat the mascarpone, cream, sugar, and vanilla together until stiff peaks.
  2. Step 2: Dip lady fingers. Add the espresso and liqueur (if using) to a shallow bowl and dip the lady fingers on both sides (don’t let them soak–just a quick dip!)
  3. Step 3: Layer mascarpone. Smooth a layer of the mascarpone/whipped cream mixture on top of the lady fingers.
  4. Step 4: Repeat. Add another layer of lady fingers (dipped in coffee and liqueur) and another layer of cheese mixture. Dust with cocoa powder.
